With the hyper-parameterization used for this demo, the proposed coding method's

  • algorithmic delay only depends on the spectrogram window size and amounts to 768 samples at 22050 Hz sampling frequency (34.8 ms).
  • The computational complexity at the encoding end is 2.02 GMAC/s.
  • The computational complexity at the decoding end is 3.50 GMAC/s.
